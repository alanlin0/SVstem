Lead a Chapter

Bring SVstem to your school and make a difference in your community.

Start a Chapter at Your School

Are you passionate about STEM education and want to make a real impact? Starting an SVstem chapter at your high school is a great way to lead outreach efforts, build leadership skills, and help provide quality STEM resources to underprivileged communities in your area.

As a chapter leader, you'll organize events, recruit volunteers, and work directly with students who need access to STEM education. You'll be supported by our central team every step of the way.

What You'll Need

  • A team of at least 3 dedicated student volunteers
  • A faculty advisor at your school
  • A passion for STEM education and community outreach
  • Commitment to hosting at least one event per semester

How to Get Started

  1. Reach out to us at silvalstem@gmail.com expressing your interest
  2. Assemble your founding team and find a faculty advisor
  3. We'll schedule an onboarding call to walk you through everything
  4. Get approved and start planning your first event!
